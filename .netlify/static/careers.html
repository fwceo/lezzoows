Our Values

What Drives Us

⚡

Move Fast

We shipped our first product in weeks, not months. Speed is in our DNA — it's in our name. Quick decisions, rapid iteration, bias toward action.

🌍

Build for Iraq

We don't copy-paste Silicon Valley playbooks. We build for the realities of our market — cash economies, infrastructure gaps, and a young population hungry for innovation.

🤝

One Team

From engineers in Erbil to riders on the streets — we're one team. No egos, no silos. The person who coded Lezzoo's first feature also delivered its first order.

🎯

Think Big, Start Small

Our vision is to build Iraq's digital infrastructure. But we get there one order, one product, one city at a time. Ambition grounded in execution.

🧪

Experiment Relentlessly

Trivia, Scrolls, Jiran, Xomali AI — each started as an experiment. We launch fast, learn faster, and double down on what works.

❤️

Kurdish Pride, Global Standards

We're proudly from Kurdistan. Our name is bilingual Kurdish. But our standards are global. Y Combinator, world-class UX, and tech that competes with anyone.
